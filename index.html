Ralph (the organ donor)

  • High Concept: All the sad musicians of the world wallow, as Ralph (a man with an abundance of organs) attempts to save them from themselves. Donate an organ to the musicians to increase your score, and if they fall on you you die!
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should have fun with the physics of the game and also have a good laugh. The sound effects are particularly interesting for this game as well.
  • Genre: platformer-ish
  • Game play: left, right and up arrow keys. Space button to shoot.
  • Prototype goal: nail down group physics, sprite sheet animation, and game states.
  • Features: cool music, a funny theme, good physics.
  • Setting/Premise: The setting is a grand stage, where our musicians hoped they'd perform. The premise is to save them from their sorrows by donating to them a brand new organ!
  • Story: All the sad musicians of the world wallow, as Ralph (a man with an abundance of organs) attempts to save them from themselves. Donate an organ to the musicians to increase your score, and if they fall on you you die!
  • Target Audience: gamers of all ages
  • Play time: 4-6 minutes
  • Strategies: move back and forth as fast as possible!
  • Prototype Assets: google images for the background and Ralph, I drew the musicians and game over, open source sound files.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: space invaders
